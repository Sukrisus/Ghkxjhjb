What is Miori?

Future of MCPE

mioriPE is a closed source client-side modded version of Minecraft PE designed to enhance performance, improve visuals, and add customization features, similar in spirit to OptiFine Or other popular Java Mods like Sodium, Vulkan, etc.

MioriPE was made for mobile players to have more freedom to their game, it is not a client and will never be one, i can installed through the MioriPE installer
